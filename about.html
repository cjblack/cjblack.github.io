about

neuroscientist/engineer/programmer

I am a PhD student at Brown University working in Biomedical Engineering. However, as most BME students, my work is quite interdisciplinary. Using a mixture of neuroscience, engineering, and programming techniques, I am working on sensory models to develop novel tools that can one day be translated to the clinic.

Currently, my focus is in spinal and cortical processing of sensory information. To better understand such processing, I am working on developing a large-scale neural framework written in NEURON & Python. I will be using this pages site to add any relevant information I've come across to help others with their own coding and experimentation. Additionally, I will be using this page to explore HTML, CSS, and Javascript, so please bear with me...
